## What to do
### 1. Download [config.json](../swaync/config.json) and [style.css](../swaync/style.css) files from this repo

### 2. Paste the files into your ~/.config/swaync/ directory

### 3. Reload swaync client
```shell
swaync-client -R && swaync-client -rs
```
